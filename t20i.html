T20 International Career of Shoaib Akhtar

Shoaib Akhtar played 15 T20 Internationals (T20Is) between 2006 and 2010, capturing 19 wickets at an average of 22.73 and a strike rate of 16.7. He maintained an economy rate of 8.15, with best figures of 3/38 against England in 2010. Notably, he was a key spearhead during the inaugural 2007 ICC World T20, taking six wickets to help Pakistan reach the final.
