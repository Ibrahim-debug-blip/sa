First Class Career of Shoaib Akhtar

Shoaib Akhtar enjoyed a highly impactful first-class career spanning from 1994 to 2009, capturing 467 wickets in 133 matches at an impressive average of 26.26. Known for his terrifying pace, the "Rawalpindi Express" represented prominent domestic teams including Rawalpindi, Pakistan International Airlines (PIA), and Khan Research Laboratories (KRL), alongside English county stints with Somerset, Durham, Surrey, and Worcestershire. His domestic red-ball tenure featured 28 five-wicket hauls and 2 ten-wicket match hauls, showcasing his ability to dismantle top-tier batting lineups even when battling recurrent injuries.
