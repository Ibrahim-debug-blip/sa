Test Career of Shoaib Akhtar

Shoaib Akhtar's Test career spanned 46 matches from 1997 to 2007, capturing 178 wickets at a stellar average of 25.69 and a strike rate of 45.70. The speedster claimed 12 five-wicket hauls and two ten-wicket match hauls, featuring a career-best 6/11 against New Zealand in 2002. Renowned for terrifying spells, his iconic 1999 Asian Test Championship performance in Kolkata saw him bowl Rahul Dravid and Sachin Tendulkar on consecutive deliveries, cementing his legacy as a premier red-ball match-winner.
