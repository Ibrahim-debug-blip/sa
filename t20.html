T20 Career of Shoaib Akhtar

Shoaib Akhtar featured in 38 domestic T20 matches between his format debut for Durham in 2003 and his final appearances in 2010. He captured 44 wickets at a strong average of 22.23 and maintained an economy rate of 7.30. Representing regional teams like the Rawalpindi Rams and Islamabad Leopards, his career-best domestic T20 bowling figures of 5/23 were dismantled against the Quetta Bears. Additionally, during his brief but memorable 2008 IPL stint with the Kolkata Knight Riders, he produced a legendary, match-winning spell of 4/11 against the Delhi Capitals.
